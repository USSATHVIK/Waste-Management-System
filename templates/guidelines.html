About EcoTrack

Our Mission

EcoTrack is a comprehensive waste management platform designed to empower individuals and communities to make sustainable environmental choices.

Key Objectives

  • Promote responsible waste disposal and recycling
  • Encourage sustainable waste management practices
  • Track and reward individual environmental contributions
  • Reduce carbon footprint through intelligent waste tracking
How EcoTrack Works

  1. Log your waste collections
  2. Get points for recycling
  3. Track your environmental impact
  4. Earn rewards and incentives
